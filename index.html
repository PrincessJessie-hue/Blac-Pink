About Me

Software & Sorcery

I am a Pretoria-based UI/UX designer with a background in coding and a deep love for systematic thinking. I believe the best products feel inevitable like they could not exist any other way.

  1. 01
    Clarity over Cleverness
    Real design intelligence is measured by how little explanation a product needs.
  2. 02
    Constraints are Creative Fuel
    Edge cases and accessibility requirements reveal opportunities, not blockers.
  3. 03
    Ship, Then Refine
    A good product in users' hands beats a perfect product on a Figma canvas.
  4. 04
    Empathy is a Design Tool
    Deep user research is the single highest-ROI activity in any design process.
