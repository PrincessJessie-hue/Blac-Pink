Project Overview

A simple solution for daily mindfulness.

Many people struggle with stress but find existing meditation apps overwhelming or difficult to use. Calmora explores a simple and user-friendly solution to encourage mindfulness in daily life, meeting users where they are rather than where an app expects them to be.

Problem Statement

Many users struggle to maintain daily wellness habits due to busy schedules and lack of guidance.

Goal

Design an intuitive mobile app that encourages consistent wellness routines and promotes mental well-being.

Prioritisation

Eisenhower Matrix

Features were mapped across four quadrants based on urgency and importance, ensuring the core wellness tools were scoped into the MVP and non-essential features were deferred or cut entirely.

Urgent
Not Urgent
Important Do Now
  • ●One-tap daily mood check-in
  • ●Guided breathing exercises (under 5 min)
  • ●Lora AI therapist chat feature
  • ●Onboarding flow and user profile setup
Important Schedule
  • ●Mood history and progress visualisation
  • ●Personalised session recommendations
  • ●Guided sleep meditations
  • ●Manage Emotion toolkit with definitions
Not Important Delegate
  • ●Push notification logic and scheduling
  • ●Backend data syncing and storage
  • ●App store listing and screenshots
Not Important Eliminate
  • ●Social sharing and leaderboards
  • ●Gamification badges and streak counters
  • ●Community forums and user posts
  • ●Video content library (v1 scope cut)
Key Decision Streak counters and social features were deliberately eliminated in Q4. Research showed they were the primary driver of guilt and app abandonment, the opposite of what Calmora is designed to do.
